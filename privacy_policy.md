# VR Bronchoscopy Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 11/5/2025

Welcome to VR Bronchoscopy! This policy explains what information we collect when you use our VR application, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect (And Why)

We keep data collection to a minimum, focusing only on what is necessary for the application to function and improve.

| Data Category | Specific Examples | Why We Collect It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data Provided by Meta Platform** | Your Meta User ID, Profile Name (from your Meta/Oculus account). | To allow you to log in and access the application. |
| **VR Usage Data** | Time spent in the app, features used, and application performance (crashes, errors). | To understand how the app is used, fix bugs, and improve the application's stability and features. |
| **VR Tracking Data (Important for VR Apps)** | Head and hand movement/position within the application environment. | **Crucially, this data is used *only* for the core functionality of the VR Bronchoscopy simulation (e.g., positioning your virtual view and tools). We DO NOT use this data to identify you or create a biometric profile outside of the simulation.** |

---

## 2. We Do Not Collect:

* **Personal Health Information (PHI):** Since this is a simulation, we do not collect any real-world patient or health data.
* **Audio/Microphone Recordings:** We do not record or store your voice.
* **Your Real Name or Email:** Unless you contact us directly for support.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell any of your data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

* **Meta Platforms:** We share data like your Meta User ID and app usage information with Meta Platforms, Inc. (the operator of the Meta Store) as required by their policies for platform functionality and safety. Meta has its own separate privacy policy that governs its use of this data.
* **Service Providers:** We may use third-party services (e.g., a service to track application crashes) that may access Usage Data, but only as necessary to perform their services for us. They are legally bound to protect your data.
* **Legal Requirements:** If we are required to do so by law (e.g., a court order or subpoena).

---

## 4. How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.
